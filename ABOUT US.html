Welcome to Kazisafi Workshop – Automotive Painting Experts!

Kazisafi Workshop is a modern auto workshop specializing in high-quality vehicle painting services. With skilled professionals and advanced tools, we restore and enhance your car’s appearance with precision and excellence.

We are located in SIDO Industrial Area, Moshi, Kilimanjaro – directly opposite Siweto Airstrip. Whether you want a fresh coat of paint or to repair damaged surfaces, Kazisafi is your trusted solution.
